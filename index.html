About Us

Our executive committee was established in 2018. We aim forwards the pursuit of nurturing future global leaders through model united nations activities.

Working with UNESCO Hong Kong Association, we share a common objective of promoting a diversified and free culture from which people of all nationalities may be able to gain access to quality dialogue, protection to basic human rights, and most importantly the freedom of expression. ISMUNHK will be held with the teamâ€™s vision in mind to promote the United Nations' founding principles.

Our Aims

  1. To offer students an opportunity to know more about global security and politics, international economics, historical conflicts, and health issues worldwide
  2. To develop students' critical thinking and debating skills
  3. To provide students with other learning experiences for all rounded and whole person development
  4. To promote Model United Nations within Hong Kong

The Executive Committee

Coming from 4 different colleges, 14 of us are enthusiasts of Model United Nations. Our members have a wide variety of knowledge ranging from organising events to leadership skills. We hope to share our ever increasing passion in MUN with more delegates thorough DJS.
